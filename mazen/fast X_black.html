Fast and the furious X

In Fast X, Dom Toretto and his family face their most formidable adversary yet: Dante Reyes, the vengeful son of drug kingpin Hernan Reyes. After a daring heist in 2011, Dom and his crew inadvertently set off a chain of events that lead to Dante’s thirst for revenge. The film takes us on a high-octane ride across the globe, from Los Angeles to Rome, Brazil to London, and even Antarctica. New allies emerge, old enemies resurface, and everything changes when Dom discovers that his own 8-year-old son is Dante’s ultimate target. Directed by Louis Leterrier, the movie features returning cast members like Michelle Rodriguez, Tyrese Gibson, and Jason Statham, along with new additions like Brie Larson and Rita Moreno1. The final road begins, and Dom’s family must fight for their lives against their most dangerous foe yet
