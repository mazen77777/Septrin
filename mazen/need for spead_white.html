Need for speed

In the 2014 film “Need for Speed”, Tobey Marshall, a skilled street racer and mechanic, finds himself framed for a murder he didn’t commit. After serving time in prison, he seeks redemption, recognition in the racing world, and a solution to his problems. But above all, his driving force is revenge. To achieve this, he reacquires the fastest car from his workshop and enters the secretive and high-stakes race known as The DeLeon. Along the way, he faces challenges, evades the police, and confronts his ex-partner, Dino Brewster, who betrayed him. The film combines love, redemption, revenge, and motor oil in a thrilling ride across the country
